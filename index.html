Welcome to Gomavision

Gomavision Private Limited is a Kerala-based wholesale manufacturer of commercial-grade gym rubber mats and tiles engineered for durability, anti-slip safety, and high impact resistance.

We focus on long-term structural performance, affordable pricing, and sustainable rubber processing systems.

About Gomavision Private Limited

Gomavision operates as a structured rubber flooring manufacturing enterprise serving fitness centers, gyms, and industrial sectors across India.
