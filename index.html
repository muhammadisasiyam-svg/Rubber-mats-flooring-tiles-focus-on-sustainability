Welcome to Gomavision

Gomavision Private Limited is a Kerala-based manufacturer of commercial-grade gym rubber mats and tiles. We supply wholesale flooring solutions engineered for durability, anti-slip safety, and high impact resistance.

Our focus is long-term performance, structural strength, and competitive pricing for bulk buyers.

About Our Company

Gomavision Private Limited operates as a structured rubber flooring manufacturing company serving gyms, fitness centers, and industrial facilities across India.
